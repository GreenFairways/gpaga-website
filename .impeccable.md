# GPAGA Design Context

## Users
- **Primary**: Georgian golfers (amateur and professional), ages 25-55, tech-savvy, English and Georgian speaking
- **Secondary**: International golfers visiting Georgia, tournament sponsors, golf tourism operators
- **Tertiary**: R&A/EGA/IGF representatives evaluating Georgia's golf federation readiness

## Brand Personality
- **Professional yet approachable**: Serious sport organization, but young and growing
- **Ambitious**: "We're new, we're serious, and we have beautiful courses"
- **Grounded in nature**: Georgian mountains, lush fairways, golden hour light
- **Modern**: Digital-first, not a dusty old federation

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Bold and clean**: Minimal layouts with generous whitespace, no clutter
- **Nature-meets-precision**: Organic shapes (the logo's golf ball + field motif) paired with sharp typography
- **Color strategy**: Primary Green (#87AF44 / oklch 0.72 0.15 128) for energy and nature; Teal (#173B4A / oklch 0.30 0.05 220) for authority and trust. Green = accent/CTA, Teal = text/headings/structure
- **Photography**: Golden hour shots, mountain backdrops, real Georgian courses (Tbilisi Hills, Tabori, Kachreti)
- **Illustrations**: Flat-style golfer figures in brand palette (from brandbook)

## Design Principles
1. **Teal leads, green accents**: Teal is the dominant color (text, headings, nav). Green highlights CTAs, badges, active states
2. **Outfit everywhere**: Single font family (Outfit) in multiple weights. Bold for headings, Regular for body, Light for captions
3. **Asymmetric layouts**: Avoid identical card grids. Use varied section heights, offset content, hero images that bleed
4. **Purposeful motion**: Subtle entrance animations, no bounce/elastic. Staggered reveals on scroll
5. **Accessibility first**: Focus indicators, ARIA labels, prefers-reduced-motion, semantic HTML, 4.5:1 contrast
6. **No AI slop**: No purple gradients, no glassmorphism, no gradient text, no hero counter animations, no generic card grids, no cyan-on-dark

## Typography Scale
- Display: Outfit Bold, clamp(2.5rem, 5vw, 4rem)
- H1: Outfit Bold, clamp(2rem, 4vw, 3rem)
- H2: Outfit SemiBold, clamp(1.5rem, 3vw, 2.25rem)
- H3: Outfit Medium, clamp(1.25rem, 2.5vw, 1.75rem)
- Body: Outfit Regular, 1.125rem, line-height 1.6
- Small: Outfit Light, 0.875rem
- Caption: Outfit Light, 0.75rem

## Spacing Scale
Based on 0.25rem (4px) increments: 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96, 128px

## Component Patterns
- Buttons: Rounded-lg (not full), green bg for primary, teal outline for secondary
- Cards: Subtle border (--border), no drop shadow, hover: slight translate-y
- Navigation: Teal text, green underline on active, sticky header with backdrop-blur
- Sections: Alternating white/surface backgrounds for rhythm
